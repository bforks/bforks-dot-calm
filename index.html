Brittany Forks

Designer + Illustrator from San Francisco

Hello, I'm Brittany Forks. I'm a designer, illustrator, and crafter. I'm in love with the internet. I'm also a published author.

In 2009, I wrote a book with Random House called Kilobyte Couture. It's a how-to book on making fancy jewelry out of computer components. You can check it out on Amazon.

Lately, I've been illustrating more. Improving your craft should be a lifelong goal. At least, that's what I believe. Check out how my illustration skills are progressing on my portfolio or Dribbble.

When I'm not designing — I'm exploring San Francisco, polishing my nails at #nailconf, having opinions on twitter, or posting pics on instagram.

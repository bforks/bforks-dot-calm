Hey y'all,

I'm Brittany Forks. I'm a designer, illustrator, published author, crafter, and internet lover. I love to design neat things and think about them.

In 2009 I wrote a book with Random House called Kilobyte Couture. It's a how-to book on making your own nerdy baubles out of computer components. Lately I've been exploring illustration a little more since I think improving your craft should be a lifelong goal. Sometimes I post what I draw on my portfolio or Dribbble.

When I'm not designing—I'm exploring San Francisco, polishing my nails at #nailconf having opinions on twitter, or posting inspiring pics on tumblr.

Love,
